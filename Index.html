Frequently Asked Questions

What is temporary email?

A temporary email is a short-lived address that does not require registration. Use it to receive activation emails or download resources without cluttering your main inbox.

How long does the email last?

Each email address generated on TempInbox is valid for 15 minutes. Once the timer reaches zero, the address is deactivated and all contents deleted.

Is TempInbox free?

Yes, TempInbox is 100% free to use. We support the service through minimal, non-intrusive advertisements.

Is it anonymous?

Yes, we require no personal data or registration. We do not store logs of who generated which email address.
